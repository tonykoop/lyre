# Supplier RFQ

## Questions To Ask

### Tonewood and hardwood suppliers

- What clear stock sizes are available for walnut, cherry, maple, mulberry,
  and spruce suitable for this lyre scale?
- Can they supply matching billets for paired arms and yoke stock?
- What moisture content and grain orientation can they guarantee?

### String suppliers

- What off-the-shelf or custom sets best support a 13-string E4-E5 or D4-D5
  lyre with near-equal speaking lengths around 21.5 in?
- What total tension range should be expected for gut, Nylgut, or sugarcane?
- Are mixed-material sets recommended across the octave?

### Pickup suppliers

- Which low-mass contact or internal pickups work well on small wooden bowls or
  lyre-family instruments?
- What is the smallest reversible jack or connector strategy they recommend?

### Hardware and leather suppliers

- What lightweight strap solutions avoid heavy visible metal?
- Which leather, felt, cork, or suede materials perform best as removable lap
  pads without transferring dye or residue?
