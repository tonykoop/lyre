# CNC Setup Sheet

## Scope

This is a pre-CAM setup packet for lyre fixtures, sample boards, and prototype
components. It is not G-code.

## Shop Assumptions

- small router or CNC capable of wood and fixture work;
- hand finishing available after machining;
- peg and joinery accuracy matter more than decorative throughput.

## Datums

- Datum A: soundboard/string plane centerline.
- Datum B: lower anchor reference.
- Datum C: yoke axis / peg-line reference.

## Workholding Priorities

- keep thin soundboard coupons fully backed;
- use sacrificial spoilboard for bridge and tailpiece blanks;
- fixture yoke blanks to preserve cylindrical reference before final shaping;
- do not machine final player-contact surfaces to finished feel.

## Release Checks

- verify toolpath does not enter active bridge field on soundboard pieces;
- verify joinery coupons leave enough material for post-machining hand fitting;
- verify peg-hole fixture repeats angle and spacing before touching final stock.
