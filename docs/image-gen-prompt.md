# Image Generation Prompt

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of a compact 10-string plucked lyre prototype with a flat soundboard, two hardwood arms, crossbar/yoke, individual tuning pins, smooth bridge and nut contact surfaces, and a simple C4-to-E5-style diatonic string fan implied without labels. Show continuous grain through the arms where possible, clean joinery, natural oiled hardwood frame, spruce or cedar-like soundboard, nylon or fluorocarbon strings, and a small bench-buildable scale. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, about a 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop scene of the lyre on a bench during gradual pitch-up, with a tuner, spare monofilament strings, a scrap-block tuning-pin torque test, and notes about yoke movement nearby. Natural light, a player lightly plucking one string while the frame remains supported, calm prototype-validation mood.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the lyre crossbar/yoke and tuning pin row, showing individual strings bending smoothly over the nut, hardwood grain direction through the loaded member, clean pin holes, and a tiny witness mark for movement under soft raking light.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, no watermarks, no metal fantasy lyre, no impossible string fan, no short-grain structural failure shown as normal, no extra hands.
