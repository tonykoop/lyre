Family Overview

Lyre Variants

The lyre packet starts with four linked configurations so the project can scale by string count, body size, ergonomics, and joinery strategy without abandoning one master CAD workflow.

Members

  • LYR-07-HERMES: historically conservative 7-string reference.
  • LYR-13-MULE: lower-risk structure and voicing prototype.
  • LYR-13-WANDERER: main visual and acoustic target.
  • LYR-13-WANDERER-ERG: ergonomic and stage-support variant.

Shared Logic

All four members share one governing geometry strategy: equal or near-equal speaking lengths, a shell-like body, rising arms, a cylindrical yoke, visible lashing, and a common global-variable namespace in SolidWorks.
