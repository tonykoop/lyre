# E Dorian Scale Walk

**Provenance:** Original exercise.

**Range:** E4–E5 (one octave). All notes lie on open strings in the standard
Dorian lyre tuning.

**Practice tips:**
- Play slowly at ♩=60 first. The second section jumps by thirds — feel the skip
  before you play it.
- Accent the F♯ gently each time: it marks the boundary between natural minor
  and Dorian.
- Once comfortable, try stopping each note short (non-legato) for a more
  period-accurate plucked sound.
