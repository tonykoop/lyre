# Greensleeves

**Provenance:** Traditional English ballad, first referenced in 1580 (Stationers'
Register, London). Melody is anonymous. Public domain.

**Range:** D4–C5. Played in A Dorian/Aeolian on the lyre.

**Practice tips:**
- The 3/4 waltz feel comes from emphasizing beat 1 of each bar with a slightly
  fuller pluck.
- The F♯ in bar 7 (marked ^F in ABC) is the only accidental — it gives the tune
  its characteristic Dorian lift just before the phrase ends. Don't miss it.
- Slow it down to ♩=40 and play it as a lament; speed it to ♩=80 and it becomes
  a dance. Both are historically appropriate.
