# Aegean Shore

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this lyre build.
MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's lyre (E Dorian, 7-string build).

**Range:** E4–E5. Stays on open strings throughout the A section; the B
section climbs to the top octave.

**Form:** AABB (repeat each section once for a full performance).

**Practice tips:**
- The A section is mostly stepwise — a good test of your pluck evenness.
- In the B section the melody descends from high D before climbing back; feel
  the arch.
- At performance tempo (♩=92) this should sound like a slow Mediterranean dance.
  Don't rush the final long E — let it ring and damp naturally.
