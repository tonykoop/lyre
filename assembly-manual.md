# Assembly Manual

## Prototype Sequence

1. Build and verify `LYR-000_MasterLayout` from the equations file and family
   spec.
2. Produce shell, soundboard, arms, yoke, bridge, and tailpiece blanks.
3. Fit arm sockets and hidden registration features before final surface
   finishing.
4. Dry-fit yoke saddles, peg spacing, and lashing geometry.
5. Prototype lap saddle and rear-hand relief on the mule before committing the
   main body.
6. Final-fit bridge and tailpiece geometry and verify string path.
7. String the mule at staged tension and inspect deflection.
8. Add pickup and strap experiments only after the acoustic baseline is known.
9. Reserve final inlay and stand refinement for the post-validation build.

## Inspection Gates

- Joint fit before glue or mechanical locking.
- String plane clearance before peg finalization.
- Body comfort before finish.
- Acoustic response before ornament.
- Service access before pickup or strap hardware lock-in.
